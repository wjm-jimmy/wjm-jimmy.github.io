Jiamin Wang

Control Science and Engineering, Xidian University

Hi, I’m a fourth-year ph.D. candidate in the School of Mechano-electronic Engineering at Xidian University, and my supervisor is Professor Yuanshi Zheng. I received the B.S. degree from Chang’an University in 2019. I have been taking the successive master-doctor program for the Ph.D. degree since March 2021.

My research interests include robust control, multi-agent systems, complex networks, and collective intelligence. Please feel free to contact me at wangjiamin21@outlook.com if you would like to discuss possible collaboration or shared interests.
