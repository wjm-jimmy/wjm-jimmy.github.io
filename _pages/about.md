Hi, I'm a third-year ph.D. student in the School of [Mechano-electronic Engineering](https://emen.xidian.edu.cn/) at Xidian University, and my supervisor is Professor [Yuanshi Zheng](https://web.xidian.edu.cn/yszheng/index.html). I received the B.S. degree from Chang'an University in 2019. I have been taking the successive master-doctor program for the Ph.D. degree since March 2021.

My research interests include robust control, multi-agent systems, complex networks, and collective intelligence. Please feel free to contact me at wangjiamin21@outlook.com if you would like to discuss possible collaboration or shared interests.
